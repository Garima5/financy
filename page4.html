Debit cards allow you to spend the money you have saved at the bank, so when you make a purchase, it immediately deducts from your account. Debit cards can easily fit in your wallet.

You have to be at least 18 years old to get your own credit card. Paying with a credit card is more convenient because it can easily fit in your wallet. With credit cards, you purchase items now and pay for them in the future in your bill. Be careful not to overspend or else you can’t pay your bill!

Paying with cash makes sure you don’t overspend since you are only spending what you actually have.
